Important Information for Passengers

Please read the following information carefully before your travel.

Security Information

Arrive at least 2 hours before domestic flights and 3 hours before international flights. Ensure all personal belongings are screened.

  • Carry your boarding pass and ID at all times.
  • Remove electronic devices from your luggage for screening.
  • Follow instructions from security personnel.

Baggage Information

Check your baggage allowance with your airline. Different airlines have varying rules on size, weight, and number of bags.

  • Hand luggage should not exceed 7 kg.
  • Liquids in carry-ons must be under 100 ml and packed in a resealable bag.
  • Label all checked luggage with your name and contact details.

Prohibited Items

Certain items are prohibited in both hand and checked luggage for safety reasons:

  • Explosives, fireworks, and flammable liquids
  • Sharp objects like knives and scissors
  • Guns, ammunition, and other weapons

Travel Tips

Here are some tips for a smoother travel experience:

  • Keep copies of your travel documents (passport, visa, etc.).
  • Stay hydrated and move around to avoid discomfort during the flight.
  • Double-check your boarding gate and time.
  • Download your airline's app for flight updates.
